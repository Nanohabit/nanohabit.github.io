Apple Watch Apps

Simple, private, and fast.

Nanohabit builds focused Apple Watch apps that respect your time and your data. WorkTap helps you complete your work hours without complexity. WatcH2O helps you track daily water intake. BioBattery shows your social energy level. NUR delivers timed Quranic verses throughout your day.

NUR

NUR displays Quranic verses on your Apple Watch every 3 hours, designed to bring light, reflection, and tranquility to your day.

  • Verse notification every 3 hours
  • Minimal and distraction‑free
  • No accounts, no tracking
  • Everything stays on your device
Why I Built NUR

I wanted a silent spiritual companion on the wrist, without apps that distract or overwhelm. NUR brings short moments of reflection throughout the day—without needing your phone.

No analytics, no online services, no identity. Just light.

Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

This privacy policy applies to WorkTap, WatcH2O, BioBattery, and NUR. None of these apps collect, store, or transmit personal data to external servers.

Work Data (WorkTap)

WorkTap stores your sessions locally. No data is sent to analytics or third parties.

Health Data (WatcH2O only)

If enabled, WatcH20 writes water intake to Apple Health. Data stays on your device only.

Spiritual Data (NUR)

NUR does not collect or store any personal information. Verses are displayed locally on your device. No data leaves your Apple Watch.
